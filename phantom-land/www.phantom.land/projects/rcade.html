ABOUT

Revolving Games are redefining decentralised gaming with Rcade, a hub connecting their growing universe of Web3 experiences. We created a digital world that captures the energy of their vision, an interactive, high-fidelity space that excites players while delivering a seamless experience for investors.

Before launch, we built anticipation with a cinematic holding page featuring an unpowered node, pulsing with potential and waiting to be activated. Designed to intrigue and excite, this teaser drew users in, setting the stage for Rcade’s full reveal.

At launch, the site transformed. The node powered up, revealing a hyper-realistic WebGL world filled with animated interactions, immersive sound design, and retro-futuristic arcade machines. We brought Rcade to life by integrating Unreal Engine assets into a seamless browser experience, balancing bold cyberpunk aesthetics with effortless navigation. Every detail, from flickering neon lights to hover-based interactions, was crafted to feel like an extension of the gaming worlds it represents.

Highlights – An interactive Web3 experience blending cutting-edge aesthetics with seamless usability, creating a premium destination for both players and investors.
