ABOUT

Groundbreaking insights deserve an experience to match. We partnered with Google Cloud to reimagine their 2024 Data & AI Trends report as an interactive, content-first experience that makes industry-shaping research feel accessible, engaging, and built for exploration.

Packed with cutting-edge data and forecasts, we designed a content-first digital report that allows users to explore insights at their own pace, scanning key takeaways or diving deep into the details through an intuitive and interactive format.

The platform distils complex material into sharp, confident narratives that are as engaging as they are informative. Layered with bold visuals, seamless animation, the experience brings Google Cloud’s expertise to life in a way that feels natural, exciting, and built for discovery. Created for longevity, the experience evolves with Google Cloud’s ongoing research and thought leadership.

Highlights – A benchmark-setting interactive report that makes Google Cloud’s AI and data insights more accessible, impactful, and engaging than ever before.
