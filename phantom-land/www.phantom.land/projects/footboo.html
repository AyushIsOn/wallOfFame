Conjuring up Euros team spirit with an AI‑powered football card generator.

ABOUT

Football fandom is as much about nostalgia as it is about the game itself. For the Euros, we created Footboo, Phantom’s Manifest AI-powered football card generator that let fans see themselves as legends from the 80s, 90s, and 00s. Instantly iconic haircuts, outrageous kits, and a bit of AI magic turned every selfie into a hilariously shareable keepsake.

The best viral experiences are quick, simple, and just unexpected enough to be irresistible. With FootBoo, that meant simply uploading a selfie, picking an era and skin tone, to be delivered a gloriously retro football card in seconds. Whether they ended up with a mullet, a centre parting, or a headband worthy of peak Beckham, every result felt like a nostalgic time capsule of footballing history.

To keep fans coming back, we dropped special edition packs throughout the tournament, celebrating everything from bobbleheads to last-minute equaliser drama. FootBoo took over social feeds, made a full segment on Lorraine, featured on Match of the Day, and even found its way onto BBC Sport.

Highlights – Over four million generations from 800,000 users, with national media coverage and viral traction across football communities, making FootBoo our most successful AI generator yet.
