A bold, kinetic identity reimagining connection at Google’s Premier Partner Summit.

ABOUT

The Premier Partner Summit wasn’t just an event, it was an opportunity for Google’s top partners to connect in a meaningful way after the pandemic. We designed a fluid, immersive identity that made every moment feel significant, guiding attendees through the event while sparking a sense of collaboration and innovation.

Inspired by the idea of time and transformation, we developed a gradient system that signified both the theme and timing of each session. These evolving colors seamlessly led attendees through workshops and talks, creating an intuitive and visually striking experience.

Beyond static visuals, we introduced movements of subtle light shifts and dynamic motion elements to reflect the energy of in-person connection. To heighten engagement even further, we composed a soundscape designed to enrich the emotional atmosphere, making the Summit feel like a celebration of progress and partnership.


Highlights – A thoughtfully designed Summit identity that redefined the event experience— guiding attendees through color, motion, and sound to create a lasting impact.
