Empowering self‑belief with a bold new identity for #IamRemarkable.

ABOUT

Talking about personal achievements can be challenging, especially for underrepresented voices in business. #IamRemarkable empowers individuals to embrace their success, but to amplify its impact, it needs a bold identity. We worked alongside #IamRemarkable’s founder to create a full rebrand, replacing the stiffness of corporate workshops with warmth, energy, and self-belief.

We built a complete visual identity from the ground up, designing a new logo, typography, and brand system that felt both personal and dynamic. A bespoke photoshoot captured participants in moments of confidence, making the brand feel deeply authentic. A clean, intuitive website made signing up seamless, while a suite of digital assets and social templates extended the initiative’s reach, equipping facilitators with the tools to inspire more individuals globally.

Handwritten typography added a final personal touch, reinforcing the initiative’s core mission: helping people put their achievements into words. Across every touchpoint, we ensured #IamRemarkable’s identity was more than just a look.

Highlights – As of December 2024, #IamRemarkable has empowered over 670,000 participants across 180 countries, with 3,000+ facilitators leading workshops worldwide to inspire confidence and self-promotion
