Experience

A beautifully illustrated digital advent calendar blending festive storytelling with exclusive Google product offers, bringing seasonal magic to life in an immersive experience.

ABOUT

We created Google Holiday Street, an interactive advent calendar revealing ten heartwarming stories and exclusive offers from daylight savings to New Year’s Day. Designed to celebrate the magic of Google devices, the experience turned festive discovery into an engaging journey.

Set on a charming illustrated street, ten interactive doors unlocked real-life moments showcasing how Google devices simplify and enhance modern living. Timed around key seasonal events like Black Friday and International Students Day, each story highlighted the Chromebook at the heart of everyday convenience.

Beyond storytelling, every door revealed exclusive Google product deals, seamlessly integrating commerce with content. This fusion of narrative and value transformed holiday shopping into an interactive experience, reinforcing Google’s role in enriching festive moments.

Highlights – A joyful, interactive way to discover the magic of Google. Delivering engagement, storytelling, and exclusive offers in one seamless experience.
